DROD (Deadly Rooms of Death) is a turn-based puzzle game where you defeat all the monsters in a room with your sword.

Play the demo of DROD: King Dugan's Dungeon at Caravel Games.

Help

Controls:

Explanation

  • The red block is the player and the gray block is their sword. Use it to strike orbs and kill roaches
  • The q and w key rotate the character
  • The numpad or YUIHJKNM. keys move the player in 8 directions
  • 5 or J are used to skip a turn without changing your position
  • Orbs manipulate doors by opening or closing them.
  • Black squares represent roaches
  • Roaches will always move after you have made your move
  • Grey squares with white squares represent Roach queens
  • Roach queens move away from the player
  • Roach queens also spawn roaches in their surrounding squares every 30 turns
  • light blue squares are stairs. You win if you touch one.
